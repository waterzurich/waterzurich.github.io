Conference venue

The Workshop will take place at the Irchel Campus of the University of Zurich (postal address: Winterthurerstrasse 190, 8057 Zurich) in room Y24-G-55.

Arrival to Zurich

Zurich can be reached by plane, train, international bus, or car. The international Zurich Airport (ZRH) is located about 8 km from the conference venue. Trains and buses arrive at Zurich main station (Zurich Hauptbahnhof), located in the city center, about 4 km from the conference venue. To find train connections, go to the website of Swiss railways: https://www.sbb.ch/. For international buses, you can check: http://www.checkmybus.com.

Arrival from the airport

When you arrive at Zurich airport, look for signs to bus/tram station. Depending on which terminal you arrive at, this will be 5-10 mins walking. There will be blue ZVV ticket machines there and you can buy one-hour ticket to Zurich for 6.80 CHF. The ticket machine takes cards and coins. You need to take the tram nr 10 in direction "Zürich, Bahnhofplatz/HB" until the stop called "Universität Irchel" (it takes 20 mins). From the tram stop, you need to cross the park to get to the campus.

Arrival from the main station

If you arrive at Zurich main station, you can take tram 7 or 14 to stop called "Milchbuck" (it takes about 10 mins) and then cross the park to get to the campus. Single ticket costs 4.40 CHF and can be purchased in blue ZVV machines that accepts cards and coins. Up-to-date information about local transportation system is available here: http://www.zvv.ch
